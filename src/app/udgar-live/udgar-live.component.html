Quiz Process

"Participants must register for the quiz through the official website."

"Upon successful registration, students will be added to their respective college WhatsApp group for further communication."

"The official quiz book, Weed Out, can be downloaded from the WhatsApp group description."

"All participants must complete their registration by 10th October."

"The intra-college quiz will be conducted online on Sunday, 13th October."

"A link to the quiz will be shared in the designated WhatsApp group."

"The quiz will take place between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM across various time slots."

"The quiz will consist of 50 questions, with a total duration of 30 minutes."

"All participants will receive detailed quiz rules and regulations a day prior to the event, which must be strictly adhered to on the quiz day."

"The top 3 winners from each college can collect their prizes on 18th October during the college fest at a designated stall, upon verification of their name, email ID, and mobile number."

"The names of the top 10 students who qualify for the inter-college quiz will be announced on 15th October via WhatsApp groups."

"The inter-college quiz, also based on the Weed Out book, will be conducted on 18th October, live on stage, with awards presented by the chief guest on the same day."
